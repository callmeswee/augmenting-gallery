introductions - this class

what? a class about the relationship between objects, spaces and information in the digital age.
why? the museum is a very specifically designed spaces which is meant to confirm the importance of what is inside it, and it both removes the context from which those things came from, and gives it a new, unique context.
how? we will take the particular angle of cutting-edge technology —augmented reality— and we will see how this technology can help us expose some aspects of objects which themselves are already exposed within a museum for their significance.
